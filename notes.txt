# CampusAI - User Guide

## Welcome

CampusAI is an AI-powered college admission assistant that helps students explore colleges, compare institutions, generate admission roadmaps, and receive AI-based admission guidance.

---

## How to Use CampusAI

### Step 1: Register

- Open the website.
- Click **Sign Up**.
- Enter your name, email, and password.
- Create your account.

---

### Step 2: Login

- Enter your registered email and password.
- Click **Login**.
- You will be redirected to the dashboard.

---

### Step 3: AI Counselor

- Open **AI Counselor Chat**.
- Type any admission-related question.

Examples:

- Tell me about IIT Delhi
- Compare IIT Bombay and BITS Pilani
- What documents are required for admission?
- What is the eligibility for Computer Science?

The AI will provide structured college information and admission guidance.

---

### Step 4: Compare Colleges

- Open **Compare Colleges**.
- Select two colleges from the dropdown lists.
- View the comparison based on:
  - Fees
  - Courses
  - Eligibility
  - Admission Process
  - Required Documents
  - Official Website

You can also export the comparison as a PDF.

---

### Step 5: Admission Planner

- Open **Admission Planner**.
- Fill in your details:
  - Preferred Course
  - Preferred State
  - Preferred City
  - 12th Marks
  - Entrance Score (Optional)
  - Category
  - Budget
  - Preferred College Type

- Click **Generate Roadmap Timeline**.

CampusAI will generate a personalized admission roadmap with recommended colleges and admission steps.

---

### Step 6: Profile

- Open **Student Profile**.
- View or update your academic preferences.
- Save the updated profile.

---

### Step 7: Logout

- Click **Logout** from the dashboard.
- Your session will end securely.

---
